# Karuna Exam System - Support Analysis & Pricing

## Support Requirements Analysis

### Critical Factors
- **One-Time Event**: No second chances if system fails
- **High Stakes**: 15,000+ students' exam results depend on system
- **7-Day Window**: Continuous operation required
- **Peak Hours**: Most students take exam during school hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
- **Indian Time Zone**: IST (UTC+5:30)

### Risk Assessment
- **High Risk**: System downtime during exam
- **Medium Risk**: Performance issues affecting user experience
- **Low Risk**: Minor bugs that don't affect core functionality

## Support Tier Analysis

### Tier 1: Basic Support
**Cost**: ₹25,000 for 7 days
**Coverage**: 9 AM - 6 PM IST (Business Hours)
**Response Time**: 2-4 hours

**Pros**:
- Cost-effective
- Covers peak exam hours
- Basic issue resolution

**Cons**:
- Limited coverage
- Slower response time
- No night support

**Best For**: Budget-conscious clients with low risk tolerance

### Tier 2: Extended Support (Selected)
**Cost**: ₹20,000 for 7 days
**Coverage**: 7 AM - 10 PM IST (Extended Hours)
**Response Time**: 1-2 hours

**Pros**:

- Fast response time

- Good value for money

**Cons**:
- No overnight support
- Higher cost than basic

**Best For**: Most clients - optimal balance of cost and coverage

### Tier 3: Premium Support
**Cost**: ₹75,000 for 7 days
**Coverage**: 24/7 for entire exam period

**Pros**:
- Complete coverage
- Fastest response time

**Cons**:
- Highest cost
- May be overkill for most situations

**Best For**: High-stakes exams or clients with zero tolerance for downtime
