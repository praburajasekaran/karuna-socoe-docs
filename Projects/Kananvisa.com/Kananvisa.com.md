# Kananvisa.com - Comprehensive Visa Portal

## Project Overview
Kananvisa.com is an e-commerce style visa application portal for Khanan's visa services. The platform offers comprehensive visa services for 6 target countries: Canada, UK, US, Dubai, Germany, and France.

## Target Countries & Visa Types
- **Canada**: Study Permits, Work Permits, Super Visa, Tourist Visa, Express Entry
- **UK**: Student Visa, Skilled Worker Visa, Spouse Visa, Visitor Visa, Global Talent
- **US**: F-1 Student, H-1B Work, B-1/B-2 Visitor, Family Green Card, K-1 Fiancé
- **Dubai**: Student Visa, Employment Visa, Tourist Visa, Golden Visa, Family Visa
- **Germany**: Student Visa, Blue Card, Schengen Visa, Family Reunion, Freelancer
- **France**: Student Visa, Work Visa, Schengen Visa, Talent Passport, Family Reunion

## Specialized Visa Categories
- **Family Visas**: Super Visa (Canada), Parents Visa, Spouse Visa, Children Visa, Fiancé Visa
- **Event Visas**: Convocation Visa, Graduation Visa, Ceremony Visa
- **Business Visas**: Investor Visa, Entrepreneur Visa, Business Visitor
- **Transit Visas**: Airport Transit, Connecting Flight Visas

## Portal Features
- E-commerce style navigation and filtering
- Country-based browsing and search
- Duration categorization (Short-term/Long-term)
- Use case categorization (Study/Work/Family/Leisure)
- Comprehensive search term optimization
- Document preparation assistance
- Expert consultation and support
- Online payment processing
- Application tracking system

## Service Model
1. Customer browses and selects visa service
2. Online payment processing
3. Document submission portal
4. Expert consultation and document review
5. Application preparation and submission
6. Status tracking and updates
7. Visa delivery and support
