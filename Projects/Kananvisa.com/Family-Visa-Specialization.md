## Common Family Visa Questions

### Frequently Asked Questions
1. **"Can I bring my parents to visit me?"**
   - Answer: Yes, through Super Visa (Canada), B-2 Visa (US), or Parent Visa (UK)

2. **"How long can my parents stay?"**
   - Answer: Varies by country - Canada Super Visa allows 2-year stays, US B-2 allows 6 months

3. **"What documents do I need for family visa?"**
   - Answer: Invitation letter, financial support proof, relationship certificates, medical insurance

4. **"Can my spouse work on family visa?"**
   - Answer: Depends on visa type - some allow work authorization, others don't

5. **"How much does family visa cost?"**
   - Answer: Varies by country and visa type - typically $299-1,999 including consultation fees
