## Core Strategy Framework

### 1. Programmatic Content Creation System

**Method**: Spreadsheet-driven content generation for scale

**Process**:
1. **Data Collection**: Create master spreadsheet with:
   - 500+ destinations (cities, countries, regions)
   - 100+ hotel types (luxury, budget, family, business)
   - 50+ travel contexts (weekend, business, honeymoon, family)
   - 25+ cashback angles (highest rates, best deals, exclusive offers)

2. **Template Creation**: Develop 10 content templates:
   - "[Destination] [Hotel Type] Booking with [X]% Cashback"
   - "How to Save on [Destination] Hotels: Best Cashback Guide"
   - "[Travel Context] in [Destination]: Maximum Cashback Strategy"

3. **Automated Generation**: Use spreadsheet formulas to create:
   - **10,000+ unique title combinations**
   - **Structured content outlines**
   - **Meta descriptions and headers**
   - **Internal linking strategies**

**Output**: 40+ unique articles per month targeting ultra-specific search queries

### 2. Video-First Content Strategy

**Inspired by**: Successful no-face YouTube channel with 2,500+ subscribers

**Channel Structure**:
- **Channel Name**: "Travel Cashback Secrets" or "Smart Travel Savings"
- **Format**: Screen recordings + motion graphics + voiceover
- **No face required**: Text overlays, animations, screen captures

**Video Types**:

1. **Quick Wins** (60-90 seconds):
   - "Get 12% Cashback on Paris Hotels - Here's How"
   - "Hidden Cashback Trick for Dubai Flights"
   - "Rakuten vs Best.so: Real Comparison"

2. **Tutorial Series** (3-5 minutes):
   - Step-by-step booking demonstrations
   - Cashback calculation walkthroughs
   - Platform comparison guides

3. **Deal Alerts** (30-60 seconds):
   - Weekly best cashback offers
   - Limited-time deal announcements
   - Seasonal travel savings

**Production Schedule**:
- **5 videos per week** (25-30 per month)
- **Repurposed across platforms**: YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok, LinkedIn

**Content Multiplication Strategy**:
