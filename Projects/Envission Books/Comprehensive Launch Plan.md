#### Day 3-5: Momentum Building
- [ ] Add additional categories while book is free
- [ ] Schedule paid promo sites (if budget allows)

### Phase 2: 99-Cent Launch (Days 6-20)
**Pricing:** $0.99 (eBook), $9.99-$11.99 (Paperback)
**Royalty:** 35% (eBook), Standard (Paperback)

#### Book Promotion Sites Strategy
**Low-Tier Sites (Days 6-8):**
- [ ] Schedule 2-3 low-tier promo sites per day
- [ ] Cost: ~$15-30 per site
- [ ] Focus on building initial momentum

**Medium-Tier Sites (Days 9-11):**
- [ ] Schedule 1-2 medium-tier sites per day
- [ ] Cost: ~$40-80 per site
- [ ] Increase sales volume

**High-Tier Sites (Days 12-20):**
- [ ] Schedule 1 high-tier site per day
- [ ] Cost: ~$100-200 per site
- [ ] Maximize final push for bestseller badge

#### Recommended Promo Sites
**High-Tier (Proven):**
- Written Word Media (Bargain Booksy) - $35-50
- BookBub - $200-400
- Fussy Librarian - $15-25

**Medium-Tier:**
- Book Gorilla - $25-40
- Ereader News Today - $20-35
- BookSends - $15-30

**Low-Tier:**
- BookScream - $10-20
- BookBasset - $15-25
- BookRaid - $10-15

### Phase 3: Full Price Transition (Day 21+)
**Pricing:** $2.99-$4.99 (eBook), $12.99-$14.99 (Paperback)
**Royalty:** 70% (eBook), Standard (Paperback)

- [ ] Switch to full pricing
