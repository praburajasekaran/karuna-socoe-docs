## Pricing Strategy Overview

Source: Sean Dollwet's official methodology 1

### Phase 1: Free Promotion (Days 1-5)

- eBook Price: $0.00 (Free)
- Royalty: 35% (KDP Select requirement)
### Phase 2: Launch Pricing (Days 6-20)

- eBook Price: $0.99
- Royalty: 35% (eBook), Standard (Paperback)

### Phase 3: Standard Pricing (Day 21+)

- eBook Price: $2.99-$4.99
- Royalty: 70% (eBook), Standard (Paperback)

---

## Psychological & Algorithmic Rationale

### 1. Amazon Algorithm Optimization

Rationale: Amazon's algorithm heavily favors books with high download and sales velocity. The free promotion creates an initial surge that:

- Improves Best Seller Rank (BSR) positioning
- Increases organic visibility in search results
- Triggers Amazon's recommendation engine
- Creates momentum for paid sales phase

### 2. Review Accumulation Strategy

Rationale: Free downloads significantly increase the likelihood of reviews because:

- Lower Risk Perception: Readers are more willing to try unknown authors at no cost
- Higher Download Volume: More downloads = higher probability of reviews
- Social Proof Building: Reviews are crucial for credibility and conversion
- Algorithm Boost: Books with more reviews rank higher in search results

### 3. Market Penetration Psychology

Rationale: The $0.99 pricing maintains momentum by:

- Price Sensitivity: Many readers have a psychological threshold under $1
- Impulse Purchase Triggers: Low price reduces decision friction
- Volume Over Profit: Focus on building readership base first
- Competitive Advantage: Undercuts typical $2.99+ pricing in most niches

## Strategic Business Rationale

### 4. Long-term Revenue Maximization

Rationale: The phased approach balances short-term volume with long-term profitability:

- Investment Phase: Free/low pricing is an investment in market presence
- Gradual Monetization: Price increases as market position strengthens
- Sustainable Growth: Builds foundation for premium pricing later
- Series Leverage: Early books support higher pricing for subsequent titles

### 5. KDP Select Enrollment Benefits

Rationale: The 35% royalty requirement enables:

- Free Promotion Access: Only available through KDP Select
- Kindle Unlimited Inclusion: Additional exposure through subscription reads
- Countdown Deals: Future promotional opportunities
- Exclusive Distribution: Amazon prioritizes Select-enrolled books

### 6. Market Positioning Strategy

Rationale: The pricing creates optimal market positioning:

- Competitive Differentiation: Stands out from higher-priced competitors
- Accessibility: Removes price barriers for new readers
- Authority Building: Reviews and sales volume establish author credibility
- Series Foundation: First book success supports entire series pricing

## Implementation Rationale for Envission Books

### 7. Self-Help/Spirituality Niche Considerations

Rationale: This pricing strategy is particularly effective for your niche because:

- High Competition: Free promotion helps break through crowded market
- Review Dependency: Self-help books rely heavily on social proof
- Series Potential: 13-book series benefits from initial momentum
- Authority Building: Reviews establish credibility in spiritual/wellness space

### 8. Cross-Promotion Benefits

Rationale: The pricing strategy enables effective series marketing:

- Lead Generation: First book attracts readers to entire series
- Bundle Opportunities: Can create series bundles at premium pricing
- Email List Building: Free downloads capture reader information
- Long-term Value: Initial investment pays off across 13-book series
