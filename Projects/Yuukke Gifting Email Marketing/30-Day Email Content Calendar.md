## Key Metrics to Track

1. **Open Rates** - Target: 25-35%
2. **Click Rates** - Target: 3-8%
3. **Conversion Rates** - Target: 2-5%
4. **Unsubscribe Rate** - Target: <0.5%
5. **Revenue per Email** - Track monthly

## A/B Testing Recommendations

1. **Subject Lines** - Test emotional vs. benefit-focused
2. **Send Times** - Test Tuesday 10 AM vs. Thursday 2 PM
3. **CTA Buttons** - Test "Shop Now" vs. "Explore Solutions"
4. **Email Length** - Test short vs. detailed versions
5. **Personalization** - Test with/without recipient names
