- **Primary**: Growth-stage company founders/CEOs (30-150 employees)
- **Secondary**: People-Ops/HR leads at startups and scale-ups
- **Tertiary**: Account managers protecting client relationships
- **Geographic Focus**: Pan-India with metro city priority
- **Industry Focus**: Tech startups, SaaS companies, consulting firms, growing service businesses

### Email Sequence (2-Email Campaign)
1. **Email 1**: Personalized Diwali gifting introduction
2. **Email 2**: Follow-up email (sent to non-responders after 2 days)

### Website Lead Capture Strategy
- **Pop-up Forms**: Diwali gifting offers and discounts
- **Exit-Intent Pop-ups**: Capture leaving visitors
- **Scroll-Triggered Forms**: Engage active readers
- **Welcome Email Series**: 3-email nurture sequence for new subscribers

- **2-Day Follow-up**: Single follow-up email to all non-responders
- **Response Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of email engagement
- **Lead Scoring**: Prioritize follow-ups based on engagement levels
- **Optimal Frequency**: 2 emails maximum to maintain deliverability

We use a **hybrid approach** that combines the best of both worlds:
