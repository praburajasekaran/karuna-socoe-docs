### Personalization Elements
1. **Company-Specific Content**
   - Industry-relevant gift suggestions

   - Local delivery capabilities

2. **Role-Based Messaging**
   - HR: Employee appreciation focus
   - Procurement: Cost-effectiveness emphasis

   - Real-time content adaptation
   - Industry-specific case studies

### Email Template Framework

#### Template 1: Personalized Diwali Introduction
**Subject**: "Hi [Name], Make This Diwali Memorable for [Company]"

**Personalization Variables**:
- Recipient name and role
- Company name and industry
- Relevant gift categories
- Local delivery options

#### Template 2: Industry-Specific Solutions
**Subject**: "[Industry] Diwali Gifts That Impress Your [Stakeholders]"

**Personalization Variables**:
- Industry-specific gift recommendations
- Stakeholder type (clients/employees/vendors)
- Company size-appropriate solutions
- Budget range suggestions

#### Template 3: Urgency-Driven Closing
**Subject**: "[Name], Last [X] Days - Secure [Company]'s Diwali Orders"

**Personalization Variables**:
- Days remaining until Diwali
- Company-specific urgency
- Custom discount codes
- Direct contact information
