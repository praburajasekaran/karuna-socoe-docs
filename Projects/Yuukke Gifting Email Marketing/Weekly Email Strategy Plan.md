# Yuukke Gifting - Weekly Email Strategy Plan
## 3-4 Emails Per Week Beyond Automation Flows

Based on Max's recommendation for 3-4 emails per week to drive retention and repeat purchases, here's a comprehensive strategy that goes beyond automation flows.

---

## Weekly Email Structure

### **Monday: Educational/Value-Driven Email**
**Purpose:** Build authority and provide value
**Goal:** Establish Yuukke as the gifting expert
**Content Type:** Tips, insights, industry knowledge

### **Wednesday: Product/Service Spotlight**
**Purpose:** Showcase specific offerings
**Goal:** Drive sales and feature specific solutions
**Content Type:** Product highlights, case studies, collections

### **Friday: Social Proof/Community**
**Purpose:** Build trust and community
**Goal:** Leverage social proof and customer stories
**Content Type:** Testimonials, success stories, user-generated content

### **Sunday (Optional 4th Email): Personal/Behind-the-Scenes**
**Purpose:** Build personal connection
**Goal:** Humanize the brand and build relationships
**Content Type:** Personal stories, company updates, founder insights

## Email Performance Metrics & Optimization

### **Key Metrics to Track:**
1. **Open Rate** - Target: 25-35%
2. **Click Rate** - Target: 3-8%
3. **Conversion Rate** - Target: 2-5%
4. **Revenue per Email** - Track monthly
5. **Unsubscribe Rate** - Target: <0.5%

### **A/B Testing Schedule:**
- **Week 1:** Subject line testing
- **Week 2:** Send time optimization
- **Week 3:** CTA button testing
- **Week 4:** Email length testing

### **Content Performance Analysis:**
- Track which content types perform best
- Monitor seasonal trends
- Analyze customer engagement patterns
- Optimize based on data

---

## Implementation Guidelines

### **Email Platform Setup:**
1. **Segmentation:** Create segments based on:
   - Customer type (B2B vs B2C)
   - Purchase history
   - Engagement level
   - Industry/company size

2. **Automation Rules:**
   - Welcome new subscribers
   - Re-engage inactive subscribers
   - Reward high-value customers
   - Personalize based on behavior

3. **Content Calendar:**
   - Plan content 4 weeks in advance
   - Create seasonal content bank
   - Develop evergreen content library
   - Schedule regular content reviews

### **Quality Control Checklist:**
- [ ] Subject line under 50 characters
- [ ] Email copy under 100 words
- [ ] Clear, single CTA
- [ ] Mobile-optimized design
- [ ] Personalization elements
- [ ] Unsubscribe link present
- [ ] Tested across email clients

### **Content Creation Process:**
1. **Research** - Industry trends, customer pain points
2. **Plan** - Align with business goals and customer journey
3. **Create** - Write copy following Max's guidelines
4. **Review** - Quality check and optimization
5. **Send** - Deploy with proper segmentation
6. **Analyze** - Track performance and optimize
