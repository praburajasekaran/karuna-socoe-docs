Karuna Club Activity Tracking & Reporting

- Current reporting system issues
  - Reports arrive daily via email
  - Information scattered across email threads
  - No systematic organization or tracking
- Required tracking elements
  - Training programs attended
  - Stories of Compassion participation
  - Conference attendance
  - Activity reports with mandatory fields:
    - Student participation numbers
    - Karuna prayer recitation
    - Pledge administration
    - Banner display during events
- Milestone recognition system
  - Track schools completing 15, 20, 30+ years
  - Automatic alerts for milestone achievements
  - Recognition gifts and certificates for long-term participants

Points-Based Recognition System (Proposed)

- Transparent credit system replacing competitive awards
  - Schools earn points for specific activities
  - Real-time point tracking visible to schools
  - Monthly communication showing current standings
- Point allocation examples
  - KC number in email subject line: 5 extra points
  - Banner display at events: additional points
  - Minimum 6 activities per year required
  - 100 points annually qualifies for Certificate of Merit
- Performance-based rewards vs. competition
  - Recognition for meeting standards rather than beating others
  - Mentor club status for 30-year veterans
  - Senior clubs train 3 new schools annually
  - Collaborative rather than competitive approach
