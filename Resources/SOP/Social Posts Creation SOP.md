## Post on Social Media

### Facebook

1. Log in to Prabuâ€™s account
2. Switch to AlgorithmShift as the poster
    
    ![image.png](Social%20Posts%20Creation%20SOP%20252f8ebd3dbe8092ad0ee48132d8aaee/image.png)
    
3. Copy the Facebook content from ChatGPT
4. Upload the image to Facebook
5. Paste the Facebook content; remove unnecessary call to action. 
6. Add the tags
7. Publish / schedule the post
8. Click on the timestamp to open the post in a popup
9. Copy the page URL 
10. Paste the URL into [Google Sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1VLfL5S9lpXENsWP44lp8Ll52ck7zkvQv0lFkdpDRk5M/edit?gid=0#gid=0)

### LinkedIn

1. Open [this](https://www.linkedin.com/company/106717655/admin/page-posts/published/?share=true) 
2. Upload image 
3. Copy the LinkedIn content from ChatGPT
4. Add paragraph breaks, bullets, etc. 
5. Ensure tags are present
6. Publish now or schedule the post
7. Open https://www.linkedin.com/company/algorithm-shift/?viewAsMember=true
8. Click the three dots in the post you just made
9. Click copy link to post
10. Paste the URL into [Google Sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1VLfL5S9lpXENsWP44lp8Ll52ck7zkvQv0lFkdpDRk5M/edit?gid=0#gid=0)
