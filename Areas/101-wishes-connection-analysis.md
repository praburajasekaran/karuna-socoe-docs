## Balancing Loops (Potential Conflicts)

### Frugality vs. Lifestyle Goals
- **Be frugal** (#32) can conflict with:
  - Luxury car (#3)
  - 5-star dining (#5)
  - Monthly gifts (#17)
  - Frequent travel (#39)

**Resolution**: Frugality in non-essentials enables spending on meaningful experiences. Prioritize experiences over things.

### Time Allocation Conflicts
- **Outstation trip every month** (#39) vs. **Business development** (#15)
- **Daily reading** (#38) vs. **Action** (#6)

**Resolution**: Outsourcing (#36) creates time for both. Early rising (#29) adds productive hours.

## Key Insights for Prioritization

1. **Start with time and energy multipliers**: Early riser, outsourcing, and health goals create more capacity for everything else.

2. **Financial goals are enablers, not ends**: The $10,000 MRR business and emergency fund unlock most lifestyle goals.

3. **Personal development compounds**: Overcoming anger and developing equanimity improve every area of life.

4. **Foundation first**: Build systems (outsourcing, frugality, early rising) before pursuing outcomes (travel, material items).

5. **Focus on loops**: Goals that create reinforcing loops (like business → resources → better business) should be prioritized.
