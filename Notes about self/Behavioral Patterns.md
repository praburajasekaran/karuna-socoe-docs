# Behavioral Patterns & Self-Observations

## Work Patterns
- **Night Productivity**: Tend to get "worked up" and complete 90% of tasks during late evening sessions
- **Communication Timing**: Conscious about not calling clients late at night, prefer morning calls
- **Delivery Preference**: Like to send completed work before lunch when possible

## Emotional Patterns
- **Mask Wearing**: Very difficult to put down mask even in intimate relationships
- **Intimacy Challenges**: Not very intimate with anyone including self
- **Self-Protection**: Always put up acceptable front, rarely share true thoughts or fears

## Task Management
- **Overwhelm Response**: When stressed, work late into night to complete tasks
- **Client Focus**: Prioritize client needs and deadlines over personal boundaries
- **Completion Drive**: High motivation to finish tasks once started
- **Risk Mitigation Planning**: Proactively thinks about backup strategies and redundancy systems
- **Strategic Implementation**: Considers multiple scenarios and failure points when planning projects
- **Expert Integration Approach**: When tasked with complex strategy, synthesizes insights from multiple industry leaders systematically
- **Comprehensive Documentation**: Creates detailed, structured documents (15,000+ words) when developing strategies for clients

## Communication Style
- **Professional Boundaries**: Maintains professional distance even in personal relationships
- **Task-Oriented**: Focuses on deliverables and outcomes in conversations
- **Self-Awareness**: Recognizes patterns but struggles to change them

## Character Type Analysis (The Laws of Human Nature)
- **Primary Type**: Personalizer - tends to take things personally
- **Challenge**: Taking feedback personally makes it difficult for others to work with or give feedback
- **Transformation Opportunity**: Convert personalizing tendency into empathy for others
- **Goal**: Use personalizing powers towards others for greater good rather than self-protection
- **Insight**: Character formation is largely inborn and difficult to change, but can be redirected

## Greene's Laws Operating in Current Patterns
- **Law of Aimlessness**: Playing small when things get difficult, lack of unifying Life-Task
- **Law of Grandiosity**: Feeling entitled without earning through apprenticeship
- **Law of Repression**: Not sharing deepest feelings, protecting others from shadow
- **Law of Narcissism**: Taking everything personally, holding long-term grudges
- **Law of Conformity**: Scattering across 20+ projects (conforming to multitasking myth)
- **Law of Self-Sabotage**: Inability to maintain habits, preferring drama of starting over to boredom of mastery

## Character Development Goals
- **Empathy Cultivation**: Transform personalizing tendency into genuine empathy for others
- **Feedback Reception**: Develop ability to receive feedback without taking it personally
- **Character Analysis**: Create system to identify character types in others for better collaboration
- **Habit Formation**: Focus on building positive character habits from the start (as habits compound)
- **Life-Task Discovery**: Find the one domain that creates obsessive curiosity and time-loss
- **Resistance Training**: Learn to embrace difficulty as necessary crucible for mastery
- **Shadow Integration**: Begin micro-disclosures to stop protecting others from humanity
